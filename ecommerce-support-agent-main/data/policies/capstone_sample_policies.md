# Capstone Sample Policies

Source: Sample_Ecommerce_Capstone_Dataset/Policies.md.

## Shipping Policy

Free standard shipping applies above INR 999. Orders below INR 999 have a flat
INR 79 shipping fee. Standard delivery takes 3 to 6 business days, with possible
longer timelines for remote locations. Orders are processed Monday through
Saturday, excluding public holidays.

## Returns and Refunds Policy

Most products are returnable within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused
and returned with accessories and packaging. Refunds are processed only after
quality check. The typical refund timeline is 3 to 7 business days.

Non-returnable examples include hygiene or personal care items, software
licenses, digital subscriptions, and items explicitly marked non-returnable.

## Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are allowed until shipment. After shipment, cancellation is not
supported. Eligible items can be returned after delivery.

## Warranty Policy

Most electronics include a manufacturer warranty of 6 to 24 months. Warranty
claims may require invoice details and product identifiers such as serial number
or IMEI.

## Payment and Security Policy

Payments are handled through secure PCI-DSS compliant gateways and encryption.
Accepted methods include cards, net banking, UPI, wallets, and Cash on Delivery
for eligible pin codes and products. Failed payment reversals normally occur
within 3 to 7 business days.

## Privacy Policy

Personal data is used only for order fulfilment and service improvement. Data
may be shared with logistics and payment partners when required for fulfilment.

## Support Policy

Customers can contact support at support@example.com.
