# Capstone Sample FAQ

Source: Sample_Ecommerce_Capstone_Dataset/FAQ.md.

## Orders and Tracking

Customers can track shipped orders from the My Orders section after logging in.
Once an order is shipped, the platform sends an email and SMS with a tracking
link and tracking ID. Common order statuses are Processing, Packed, Shipped,
Out for Delivery, Delivered, Cancelled, and Returned.

Address changes are possible only before shipment. If an order is still in
Processing status, the customer may request an address change through support
or the account dashboard.

## Shipping and Delivery

Standard shipping is free for orders above INR 999. Orders below that value
have a flat INR 79 standard shipping fee. Express shipping may have additional
charges shown during checkout.

Standard delivery normally takes 3 to 6 business days depending on location.
Metro cities may receive orders sooner, while remote locations may take longer.
Delays can occur because of courier issues, weather, or high order volume.

## Returns and Refunds

Most items are eligible for return within 7 days of delivery if unused, in
original condition, and returned with accessories and packaging. Hygiene
products, software licenses, digital subscriptions, and items marked
non-returnable may not be returnable.

Customers initiate returns from My Orders by selecting Return/Replace, choosing
a reason, and selecting refund or replacement. If eligible, a courier pickup is
scheduled.

Refunds are processed after the returned item passes quality checks. The normal
refund timeline is 3 to 7 business days depending on payment method and bank.

Damaged or wrong products should be reported within 48 hours of delivery with
clear photos. Support should prioritize replacement or refund for eligible
claims.

## Payments and Security

Accepted payment methods include credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI,
popular wallets, and Cash on Delivery for eligible pin codes.

Online payments use PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways and encryption. Support
must not ask customers for full card numbers or CVV.

If a payment failed but money was deducted, the bank usually reverses the
amount within 3 to 7 business days. If not credited back, the customer should
share the transaction reference with support.

## Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled until shipped. Once shipped, cancellation is not
supported, but eligible items may be returned after delivery.

## Warranty and Support

Most electronics include a manufacturer warranty of 6 to 24 months. Warranty
details are available on the product page and invoice.

Customers can contact support at support@example.com or through website chat.
